My Future Plans after College

Get A Good Job

I would like to get a good job so that I can pay back my student loans before they become a big hassle when I have a wife and kids to also support.

Get Married

I plan on at some point in my life getting married to a beautiful woman, who loves me and all my silly, goofy quirks.
